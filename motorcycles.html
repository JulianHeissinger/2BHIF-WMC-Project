Motorcycles

Sometimes the most dangerous "hobby". Sometimes a safe way to travel. Sometimes a way of connecting with people. Always a way of having Fun.

The History:

The first ever Motorcycle was invented in 1885. The Daimler-Reitwagen. Production ramped up during the first world war. Messengers used them to communicate with the frontline.

Harley-Davidson was the biggest manufacturer after the war, selling to 67 countries.

Nowadays, not much has changed. But there has been a shift away from two-stroke engines to four-stroke engines.

I have only shown sport-bikes, but there are several types of Motorcycles:

  • Naked Bikes:

    They have almost no fairings and a comfortable seating position. They are also some of the most affordable Bikes.

    Ducati Monster.

    Raw, iconic, and untamed.
    Is there anything else you need?
    Maybe the thrill of 150 horsepower?

    2024 Ducati Monster SP

  • Choppers:

    Made to be very comfortable over long periods of time. Prices vary a lot. They are often seen as a status symbol. They aren't made to be fast, but often have lots of torque.

    Triumph Bobber

    Comfortable for hours.
    106nm of torque.
    And a beautiful sound.

    2024 Triumph Bobber

  • Supermotos:

    Light, but fast Motorcycles. They are often more expensive than most people think. Many also available as a two-stroke. They have lots of acceleration, but a relatively low top speed, which is fine because of the lack of wind protection.

    "Husky" 701.

    The most supermoto Supermoto.
    One cylinder. 700cc. All the torque.
    It will lift the front. In all gears.

    2024 Husqvarna 701

  • Sport Bikes:

    Made for racing. Very expensive, aerodynamic Motorcycles. They have a very aggressive seating position, and lots of power (most of the time).

    Supersport 950.

    Fast, light, and beautiful.
    Is there anything else you want?
    Maybe the 300 km/h top speed?

    2024 Ducati Supersport 950

There´s a lot more than those, but they are mostly mixtures of these kinds of Motorcycles.

What is the motorcycle market like now?

Over the last 20 years there has been a shift towards extremely fast (300+ km/h) models. Most people don't bother with small 250cc bikes anymore. Electronic rider aids (like ABS, TCS, WCS,...) have saved a lot of riders, but the death rate is higher than ever. Motorcycles have also been getting as expensive as most new Cars.
